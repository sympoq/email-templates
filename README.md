# Email templates

Email notification templates provide the content and structure of communications dispatched to users. These communications serve as automated alerts for agents and customers when specific actions occur, including:

-    Submission of new requests
-    Typical actions taken by agents and customers, such as replying to tickets, ticket assignment, and closing tickets.
-    Scheduled actions, such as ticket re-assignment and escalations
-    Registration of new users within the system - agents and customers.

In addition to the standard email delivery, these messages are also displayed as pop-up notifications on the agents' dashboards.
